# Barcode Feature Characterization

- Complete lineage evidence was used as the primary input.
- CA, TA, and RA were kept as separate assay channels.
- Allele annotation stayed annotation-only and did not inflate counts.
- The full-characterization benchmark showed substantial per-section lineage
  coverage:
  - `L126_Brain_s1`: 67,970 cellbins; lineage-positive fraction `0.5043`
  - `L126_Brain_s2`: 72,884 cellbins; lineage-positive fraction `0.5432`
  - `L126_Brain_s3`: 70,155 cellbins; lineage-positive fraction `0.5712`
- Tile coverage was near complete in all three sections.
